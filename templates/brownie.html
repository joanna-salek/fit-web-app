Super-Chocolate Fit Brownie


This intense chocolate fit brownie make you feel real good after just one piece

One piece provides you only with 160 kcal and as much as 10 g of protein

LET'S COOK
{% endblock %} {% block section %}
  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Bake Time: 40 minutes
  • Total Time: 90 minutes
  • Servings: 10 pieces
  • Kcal per piece: 160 kcal
  • Bake Temperature: 180°C

Ingredients

  •   Chocolate  -  100 gram
  •   Red Beans  -  2 cans
  •   Honey  -  100 ml
  •   Eggs  -  2
  •   Erythritol  -  60 gram
  •   Cacao  -  25 gram
  •   Baking powder  -  12 gram

Preparation

    Blend beans, eggs, honey, cacao, baking powder and erythritol together until smooth. Dissolve bar of chocolate in a water bath and half of it add to the mass carefully, the other half leave for chocolate glaze. Pour the mass into a mold and bake at 180 degrees for 40 minutes. Left the cake for cooling for 30 minutes and after pour the rest of chocolate glaze on the top.
